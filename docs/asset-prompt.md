# Image prompts — icon background & social tile

The app icon is a six-segment "feelings wheel" (generated by
`scripts/generate-icons.mjs`). These prompts produce a calm **background** to sit
behind that wheel, and a **social share tile**. Keep the aesthetic low-stimulation:
soft, matte, generous empty space, muted colour — nothing harsh or busy.

**Palette to reference (paste into the prompt):**
`warm cream #F5F1E8, slate #2E3440, muted gold #E6C56B, soft amber #E0A56B,
sage #8FBF9F, dusty blue #7FA8C9, muted rose #C98A8A, muted lavender #A99BC1.`

---

## 1. Icon / tile background (square, no text)

> A calm, minimal square background for a wellbeing app icon. A soft, matte
> radial glow rising from the centre on a deep slate base (#2E3440), with a
> barely-there wash of muted gold, sage, dusty blue and lavender blended
> smoothly like coloured fog — very low contrast, no hard edges, no banding.
> Lots of quiet negative space in the centre where a logo will sit. Flat,
> modern, tasteful, low-stimulation, neurodivergent-friendly. No text, no
> letters, no symbols, no wheel, no people, no clip-art, no harsh gradients.
> Square 1:1, high resolution.

Export at **1024×1024 PNG**. Also make a **light variant** (swap the slate base
for warm cream #F5F1E8, keep the colour wash even softer) if you want a light
icon background.

## 2. Social share tile (1200×630, landscape)

> A calm, minimal social banner for a wellbeing web app called "Feelings." Warm
> cream background (#F5F1E8) with a soft matte wash of muted gold, sage, dusty
> blue, rose and lavender drifting across one side like gentle coloured fog —
> low contrast, no hard edges. Large area of clean empty space for a title.
> Flat, modern, tasteful, low-stimulation, neurodivergent-friendly. No text
> baked in, no clip-art, no people, no harsh gradients. Landscape, wide.

Ask for **1200×630 PNG**. Add the wordmark yourself afterwards (keeps text
crisp), or append to the prompt:
`Include the single word "Feelings" in a calm, rounded sans-serif, dark slate,
left-aligned in the empty space.`

Save the result to **`public/social/og-tile.png`** — the Open Graph / Twitter
tags in `index.html` already point there.

---

**Tips**

- If a result looks busy or high-contrast, add: *"softer, flatter, more empty
  space, lower contrast, calmer."*
- Generate 3–4 and pick the quietest.
- The icon foreground (the wheel) stays as-is; you're only replacing what sits
  *behind* it, so keep the centre of the square background clean.
